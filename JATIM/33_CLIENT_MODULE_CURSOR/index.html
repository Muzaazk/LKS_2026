Instructions

  • Move with W/A/S/D or Arrow keys.
  • Press Space to place a bomb. Bomb explodes after 5 seconds.
  • Explosion hits 1 tile up/down/left/right (TNT increases range).
  • Brick walls can be destroyed; stone walls cannot.
  • Items can appear from destroyed brick walls: Broken Heart, TNT, Ice.
  • Broken Heart reduces lives. Ice freezes you for 5 seconds.
  • Touching a dog or explosion reduces lives. Lives start at 3.
  • Press Esc to pause/resume.
